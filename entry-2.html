THE MAGICIAN

  • #Expression
  • #Strategy
  • #Power
  • #Inspirational action
    • Start a new adventure / Take a new path / Enter a new phase

    The Magician reminds you that you have what you need to succeed beyond just a leap of faith, but you need to be proactive and draw your own strength. It encourages you to use your intelligence, talent, and skills to make your dreams come true, rather than just waiting (or hoping) for them to come true. You will be empowered by combining the physical, mental, and emotional resources presented in this card with the energy of the spiritual and intellectual realms. Therefore, it is a great opportunity to realize the ideas you are currently envisioning. The skills, knowledge, capabilities, and hard work you've built up will lead you where you want to be. So you can take action now.

    However, you must have a clear vision to achieve your goals. You need a spiritual connection to your goals and intentions. Keep in mind that this is a great opportunity to achieve higher achievements to create the future you want.

    JOB

    It's a good time to get a promotion or start a new business. You'll have a good chance to get better financially or make more money. If you're ready, you can push it with a strong will and get the output you want.

    LOVE

    People who are in a relationship can take a step forward by guaranteeing stronger commitment and happiness. If you're single, it's a good time to meet new people. And the partner by your side is serious and good progess for you. This card represents the confidence to create and act on the opportunity to meet the persone you want, make the love you want, and meet the person you want.

    MONEY

    On the economic side, this card represetns a new opportunity to make money. As it's said on the job section, you can get a promotion or start a new business, which can lead you to make more money.

    HEALTH

    If you haven't been in good health, this card means strength and recovery. It also suggests that if you are mentally exhausted, you can meet a capable therapist who can heal you. It can be seen that your health gets better and your vitality comes alive.
